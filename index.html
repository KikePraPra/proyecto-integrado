Credits

The characters and artistic aspects of Gehenna come to life thanks to a talented team of designers and artists. Each enemy, every gloomy setting, and every eerie visual detail has been meticulously crafted to immerse players in a dark and terrifying world. The artistic direction captures the game's essence, creating an atmosphere that is both haunting and captivating. From the intricate designs of the enemies to the shadowy landscapes, every element reflects the dedication and creativity of the art team, ensuring a memorable and immersive experience for players.

The Sword of Doom is a cursed relic emerging from the darkest depths of Gehenna. Wrapped in sharp thorns and covered in blood, this sword inflicts insidious pain upon its bearer with every strike. Its blade, adorned with multiple spikes and twisted in a grotesque form, exudes a pure aura of evil. Those who dare to wield it are drawn by its power, only to discover the devastating cost of their desire. The sword seems to have a will of its own, eager to consume the blood of its user and feed off their suffering.
